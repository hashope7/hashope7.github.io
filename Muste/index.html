A Journey to teh UNited States

Muste's father makes the decision to move his family to the Dutch enclave of Grand Rapids, Michigan due to encouragement from his brothers-in-law who currently live there. They even give him a small travel loan to make the journey. After boarding a small steamer to Rotterdam in the cold January weather, followed by a journey on a much larger steamer for a two-week trip ending in Ellis Island, Muste arrives in the U.S. The family then traveled by train to their new home in Michigan.
