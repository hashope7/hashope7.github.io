May 1834

The Afscheiding

A number of ministers and their congregations secede from the Hervormde Kerk , the state-sanctioned church in the Netherlands. Albertus Van Raalte is among them. The Afscheiding (or “separation”) has begun and many Dutch Christians will leave the Hervormde Kerk in the following years as it becomes more liberal.
